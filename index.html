Donald Dump

The Story:

I was online and stumbled across a restAPI called Tronald Dump. I then felt that something had to be done about this and started coding.

"Api & web archive for the dumbest things Donald Trump has ever said."

The Purpose:

Donald Dump uses OneSignal to send push notifications known as: 'The Daily Dump'.
You can even browse amongst the best, with a collection of quotes parsed from the restAPI (https://api.tronalddump.io) or tap the dump to browse random quotes.

LivsMedel (Swedish Only)

The Story:

This started off as a solo school project where we were required to communicate with matapi.se.
We were given the creativty to design and present the information however we please.

The Purpose:

Livsmedel allows you to search not only for individual food products, but even specific meals providing their nutritional values.
You can use the camera to capture images and save them your favorites.
Here you are able to add food products to the graph, where you can compare their nutritional value.

Flag Quiz

The Story:

I initially created a simple quiz app to learn objective-C. I was quite pleased with the design and figured this could be a
good app to continue developing. I am currently rewriting the app in swift and have given it a theme.

The Purpose:

Version 2.0 takes the app to another level, adding a true theme and connecting to a backend cloud service.
I'v always wanted to get an app on the AppStore but haven't come up with an idea of what to make. This seemed like a perfect opportunity.
